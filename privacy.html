Privacy Policy

Marketing · Privacy · Terms · Support · Pricing · App Store copy

Tenant Move Out Photos is designed to be local-first. The app does not require an account, developer backend, landlord portal, tracking SDK, or advertising SDK.

Property labels, move-in/move-out dates, photo notes, and evidence photos are stored on your device unless you export or share them. When you add a photo, the app stores a copy inside the app and requests a copy to your Photos library when permission allows.

Camera and Photos permissions are used only for user-requested evidence capture, import, and saving.

Website analytics

This public marketing website uses Cloudflare Web Analytics, a cookie-free analytics service, to measure aggregate page views, referrers, geography, browser/device categories, and basic performance signals. It does not use advertising cookies, behavioral ad targeting, or cross-site user profiles, and it does not give the developer access to app data you store on your device. Cloudflare may process limited technical request data under Cloudflare's own policies.
